How to Read SAIL Diagrams

Sub-Diagram (Top-Left Triangle)

A black triangle in the top-left corner indicates that this box has a nested sub-diagram. Click the box to view its details.

Characteristic Map Indicator (Bottom-Left Dot)

A dot in the bottom-left corner indicates that this element has associated Characteristic Map mappings.

Characteristic Map (Icon Area)

The icon compartment on the right represents the element's kind. Click inside the icon area to jump to the Characteristic map.

Sub-Diagram (Name Area)

The name compartment on the left shows the element name. Click on the name to jump to the sub-diagram.
