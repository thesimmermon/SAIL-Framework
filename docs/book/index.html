Why Read This Book?

You will learn how to produce documentation that facilitates decisions rather than gathering dust. From structural boundaries to process flows and machine-readable models, this book bridges the gap between business intent and engineering execution.

Architecture as AI Guardrails

AI coding agents can read code, but they need explicit architectural context to understand boundaries, responsibilities, and constraints. This book explores how SAIL gives teams a way to make that intent explicit, providing guardrails for AI-assisted development.
